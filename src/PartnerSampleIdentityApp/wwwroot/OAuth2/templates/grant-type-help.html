Resource Owner Password Grant Not Enabled

You need to enable the "Resource Owner Password Flow" for your Auth0 application:

  1. Log into your Auth0 dashboard
  2. Go to "Applications" > your application
  3. Go to "Settings" tab
  4. Scroll down to "Advanced Settings"
  5. Go to "Grant Types" tab
  6. Check "Password" option
  7. Click "Save Changes"
